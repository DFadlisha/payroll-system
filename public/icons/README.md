# App Icons

Place your app icons here in the following sizes:

## Required Icons

### For PWA & Web
- `icon-72x72.png`
- `icon-96x96.png`
- `icon-128x128.png`
- `icon-144x144.png`
- `icon-152x152.png`
- `icon-192x192.png`
- `icon-384x384.png`
- `icon-512x512.png`

### For iOS App Store
- `icon-1024x1024.png` (App Store)
- `icon-180x180.png` (iPhone @3x)
- `icon-120x120.png` (iPhone @2x)
- `icon-167x167.png` (iPad Pro)
- `icon-152x152.png` (iPad @2x)

### For Android Play Store
- `icon-512x512.png` (Play Store listing)
- Adaptive icons (foreground + background layers)

### For Huawei AppGallery
- `icon-512x512.png`
- `icon-216x216.png`

## Design Guidelines

The MI-NES logo should:
- Use brand colors: Indigo (#4F46E5) as primary
- Be simple and recognizable at small sizes
- Have adequate padding for iOS rounded corners
- Work on both light and dark backgrounds

## Generating Icons
